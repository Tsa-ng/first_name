My First Day At A Tech School

A whirlwind of emotions filled me as I woke up on June 4th. Excitement bubbled for the tech adventure that dawned before me, while nervousness fluttered in my stomach. Having long dreamt of forging a path in the digital world, discovering SQI, a tech school renowned for its flexible learning approach, further stoked the flames of my passion. This felt like the perfect launchpad to propel me into the exciting and ever-evolving world of tech.

I got there at 8:10 am and approached the receptionist with a wave of relief washing over me. Explaining my tardiness on opening day and inquiring about my assigned class, I was met with a disarming smile and genuine friendliness. She then led me to my classroom, where I settled into a seat, the room buzzing with a diverse group of individuals, each with their own unique tech journey about to begin.

At exactly 10:00 am sharp, the instructor walked into the classroom and confidently set up his laptop. He looked around the room and noticed a few new faces, including me! With a friendly smile, he welcomed everyone and explained that he would be reviewing the material from the previous day to make sure everyone was on the same page.

My First day in tech school was not just about learning new skills but it was about embarking on a transformative journey of self discovery and exploration.
